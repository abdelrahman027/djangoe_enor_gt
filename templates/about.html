Our Story

Enor was born in 2020 when two former collegiate athletes — frustrated by gear that failed under pressure — set out to build better.

Starting in a garage workshop in Cairo, we prototyped wrist wraps, resistance bands, and recovery tools that *actually lasted*. Today, Enor serves thousands of athletes across the Middle East and beyond — but our roots remain in performance, integrity, and passion.

“If it doesn’t help you train harder, recover faster, or feel more confident — it doesn’t leave our lab.”
